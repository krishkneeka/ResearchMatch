About

Empowering students. Connecting research. Shaping the future.

Research Match is a platform designed to bridge the gap between undergraduate students and research-active professors. Our goal is to make research more accessible, discoverable, and personalized for students who want to make a difference through academic exploration.

What We Do

  • Let students search academic interests (like AI, Neuroscience, or Sustainability)
  • Display professors who are conducting research in those areas
  • Show relevant project info, fields, and required skills
  • Allow students to express interest directly through the platform
