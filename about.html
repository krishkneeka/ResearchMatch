About Research Match

Empowering students. Connecting research. Shaping the future of innovation at UTD and beyond.

Research Match bridges the gap between undergraduate students and professors by creating a dynamic platform to explore, connect, and collaborate. We believe students shouldn’t have to dig through department pages — research should be discoverable, accessible, and aligned with your goals.

What We Offer

  • Browse a directory of 1,200+ professors from all departments
  • Filter by interest area and reach out directly to faculty
  • Auto-generate personalized emails expressing your interest
  • Explore faculty titles, roles, and background information
  • Match yourself to research that inspires you
